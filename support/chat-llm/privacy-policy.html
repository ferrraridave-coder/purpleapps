Privacy Policy for Chat LLM

Last updated: 7 December 2025

Chat LLM is developed and maintained by David Picknell ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data and what information is transmitted to external services.

1. Information You Provide

Chat LLM allows you to input and upload:

  • Text prompts
  • Images
  • Documents (for example, text or PDF files)

This content is used only to generate responses from third-party AI providers. Chat LLM does not store your prompts, images, or documents on its own servers.

2. How Your Data Is Used

When you send a message or upload content, Chat LLM securely transmits this data to third-party AI services such as OpenRouter and/or OpenAI, depending on the model you select. These services process your request and return an AI-generated response, which is displayed in the app.

Chat LLM does not:

  • Store your prompts or uploaded content
  • Create or manage user accounts
  • Collect analytics or tracking data
  • Sell or share data with advertisers

3. Third-Party Data Retention

While Chat LLM does not retain your content, the third-party providers may store or log data in accordance with their own policies. You should review those policies to understand how your data is handled:

  • OpenRouter: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/row-privacy-policy/

By using Chat LLM, you acknowledge that any data sent to these providers is subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

Chat LLM does not include analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or other third-party tracking tools. We do not build profiles of users or track behaviour across apps or websites.

5. Security

All communication with third-party AI providers is transmitted over HTTPS using industry-standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely risk-free, and you should avoid sending highly sensitive or confidential information through the app.

6. Your Rights

Because Chat LLM does not store personal data or content, there is no user data held by us to access, export, or delete. Any data stored by third-party providers would be managed according to their policies, and you would need to contact them directly where applicable.
