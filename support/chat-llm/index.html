Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chat LLM?

Chat LLM is an AI chat client that lets you interact with a wide range of models via providers such as OpenRouter and OpenAI. You can send text prompts, images, and documents and receive AI-generated responses.

Do I need an account?

Chat LLM itself does not create an account, but you will need your own API keys from providers such as OpenRouter or OpenAI. Using those services requires an account with them.

What models are supported?

Chat LLM supports a wide range of OpenRouter and OpenAI models, including text and selected vision models. Some model types that generate images or video directly are not currently supported.

How is my data handled?

Your text, images, and documents are sent securely to AI providers (such as OpenRouter/OpenAI) to generate responses. Chat LLM does not store your prompts or uploads. For details on how those providers handle data, please refer to their privacy policies.

How do I report a bug?

Please email a description of the problem and, if possible, include screenshots or steps to reproduce it.
